Student Grade Prediction Application

Online Web App for conducting and grading Institute’s or College’s internal assessments.

The Cause

The Student Grade Prediction Application provides an Online Web Portal for both, the Students as well as the Professors for easier test assessments. The institutes can easily conduct online tests with the help of this app.

The Students are required to write/type their respective answers in given fields. The test conducted won’t be Objective based questionnaire as in Multiple Choice Questions. Teachers can conduct all Theory based exams.

The Results are automatically generated, at the end of the test. Our model studies the answer written by the student and generates a regression result. The students get marked automatically thus removing the hassle for Paper based tests.

Conducting tests on sheets of paper is too much of a hassle. A lot of time gets consumed in creating a seating arrangement for students, collecting the papers by hand, stacking them, checking them one by one, etc.

Often Students simply score less due their lack of knowledge, and interest. Students might not be knowing as "What are we supposed to write?" as an answer for the given question.

Gradez app provides assessments at the end of the test as to what was expected as the answer. Our app lets students know what definitions / terminologies they missed while answering the questions.
